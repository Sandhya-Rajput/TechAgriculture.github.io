This is our Project Hardware

IoT based System for Smart Farming

It consist “Arduino Uno” and many sensors like Temperature and Humidity sensor (DHT11), Soil Moisture sensor ,Ultrasonic sensor ( HC- SR04), Flame sensor and Buzzer.

Electric Fence around the Field

Distance Sensor With LED

Through this sensor we can detect approx. 400 cm distance , if any Obstacle (Cows, deer, and blue buck “Nilgai” etc.) detect from distance sensor than our led glows(red and green) in 28 cm distance and 14cm distance. Whenever LED glows with this automated switch button get ON and electric fence connection also get ON.

Fire in Field

Flame Sensor with Buzzer

Through this sensor we detect fire in distance up to 80cm , if fire detect then buzzer alarm rings then we get Notify fire burns in ripe crops of wheat, paddy crops and other crops.
